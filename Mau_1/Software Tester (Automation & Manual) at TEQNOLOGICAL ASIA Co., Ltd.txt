Mô tả công việc
We are looking for a skilled Software Testing Engineer to join our team and contribute to various projects, including websites and mobile applications for both internal and external clients. As a QA/QC Engineer, you will be responsible for ensuring the quality and performance of mobile applications (native Android/iOS) and web applications (Front-end & API). Your key responsibilities include:

Analyzing requirements, creating test cases, and developing test plans.
Collaborating closely with the development team to clarify technical designs and ensure testability.
Conducting research and proposing process improvements to enhance testing efficiency.
Performing various testing types, including functional testing, performance testing, stress testing, and data flow validation.
Ensuring application quality and compliance with Japanese market standards.
Yêu cầu ứng viên
At least 1 year of experience in manual testing (open to fresh graduates with strong potential).
Strong knowledge of software testing processes, methodologies, and techniques.
Experience with bug tracking and task management tools.
Familiarity with QA software tools and automation frameworks is a plus
Prior experience in testing projects related to the Japanese market is highly preferred.
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
Self-motivated, proactive, and detail-oriented.
Strong teamwork and collaboration skills.
