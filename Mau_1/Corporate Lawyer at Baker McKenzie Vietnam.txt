Position: Corporate Lawyer / Legal Counsel
Company: Baker & McKenzie Vietnam
Location: District 1, Ho Chi Minh City
Salary: Competitive, based on experience

- Licensed to practice law in Vietnam (Bar Association membership required)
- Strong knowledge of Vietnamese Civil Code, Enterprise Law, Investment Law
- Excellent English communication skills (IELTS 7.0+ or equivalent)
- Strong analytical, negotiation, and problem-solving skills
- Experience with cross-border transactions is a plus
- Proficiency in legal research databases (LexisNexis, Westlaw)
- Attention to detail and ability to work under tight deadlines

BENEFITS:
